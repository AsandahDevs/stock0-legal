Delete Your stock0 Account

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time.

Option 1: Delete Your Account in the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the stock0 app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Go to Settings tab
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your request

Once confirmed, your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us:

📧 Email: noelmajola@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your account email address
  • A clear request stating you want your account deleted

What Happens After You Request Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your personal data will be removed from our systems
  • You will lose access to all app features associated with your account
Some information may be retained where required for legal or regulatory purposes.

Processing Time

We aim to process all deletion requests within 7 days.
