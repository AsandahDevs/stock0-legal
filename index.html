Privacy Policy for stock0

Effective Date: 1 March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to stock0 ("we", "our", or "us"). stock0 is a mobile application designed to help users track stock information.

We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 ("POPIA") of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Encrypted password

This information is required to create and manage your account.

2.2 Location Information (Optional)

With your permission, we may collect precise GPS location data. Location access is optional and used only to enhance app functionality.

You may revoke location access at any time through your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide stock tracking functionality
  • Provide location-based features (if enabled)
  • Maintain and improve app performance
  • Respond to user support requests

We do not sell your personal information and we do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA)

We process your personal information based on:

  • Your consent (location access)
  • Contractual necessity (account creation and app functionality)
  • Legitimate interest (security and system maintenance)

5. Data Storage and Hosting

Your data is hosted on secure servers provided by DigitalOcean (London, United Kingdom region).

Although our infrastructure is located outside South Africa, we take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information is protected in accordance with POPIA.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active or until you request deletion.

We do not retain personal data longer than necessary for providing the service or complying with legal obligations.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure HTTPS communication
  • Access control restrictions
  • Server-level security protections

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission is completely secure.

8. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a South African user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for location access
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

9. Account Deletion

You may request account and data deletion by emailing us at: noelmajola@gmail.com

We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal requirements.

10. Children’s Privacy

11. Third-Party Services

We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or third-party marketing tools. Our hosting provider processes data only for infrastructure purposes.
